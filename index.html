About BioChe

Empowering students in Rajshahi with world-class Chemistry and Biology education

BioChe is a premium educational platform based in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, dedicated to helping students achieve academic excellence. Founded by two passionate educators — Polash Vaiya for Chemistry and Aysha Apu for Biology — BioChe combines PCL and ABC into one unified platform. Our mission: make Science subjects accessible, engaging, and result-oriented.
